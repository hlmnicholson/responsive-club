Leiden Swimming Club

Make a splash with us!

Everyone is welcome here at the Leiden Swimming Club, regardless of age, gender, orientation or skill level. We don't provide swimming lessons but we do provide a safe place to practice technique, share support and meet like-minded people. If you would like to attend one of our meetups, please introduce yourself send an email to info@leidenswimmingclub.com. Be sure to include your level of experience, this is particularly important if you are a beginner swimmer as we have to ensure that we have enough experienced members to provide mentorship. Safety is paramount here at Leiden Swimming Club.

Our Team

Ge to know our key memebers here at Leiden Swimming Club, without their hard work we wouldn't be here! Our members are from varied backgrounds, with a mix of ages, genders, orientations, shapes and sizes. We firmly believe that everyone has the right to enjoying the benefits of swimming and community, regardless of who they are or what they look like. Here at Leiden Swimming Club we pride ourselves on creating a safe, welcoming space for everyone.
